How tom make your own bread.

We will star with just these four ingredients. This recipe is for about 2500 gram of bread (which I make into 2 loafes of bread and about 16 buns).

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sourdough
  1. The first step is to freshen up you sourdough. You should only have about a tablespoon of sourdough in your jar. Then add 150 grams of water and 150 grams of flour and stir.
  2. When your sourdough is starting to come alive and bubbles then it's time to start the dough. First of you start with mixing flour and water.
